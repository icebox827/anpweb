== Description ==

= Floating News Headline - Scrolling Text = 

You can float new coupon codes with floating news headline.

You can float new offers with floating news headline.

You can show new of your site, It has a floating animation effect so it will float on your site.

When a viewer want to read floating news, he need to use pointer hover on the floating new then it will stop and viewer can read it.

Floating News Headline is Easy And Powerful floating or scrolling text plugin for wordpress.



**Features**
* Floating Coupons
* Floating Offers
* Floating News
* Floating Headline
* Headline Customization

==Installation==

Install Floating News Headline either via the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the files to your server.

After activating Floating News Headline, go to plugin menu.

Click Add Floating News Headline and customize your headline.

Publish the Headline and copy Floating News Headline shortcode from the bottom of the add images box and embed shortcode on any Page/Post/Text Widget.

That's it. You're ready to go!
